short answers for your questions

why learn things ?

Learning is essential because it enhances our understanding of the world, develops new skills, and allows us to solve problems more effectively. It also promotes personal growth, adaptability, and the ability to make informed decisions, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and successful life.

can you start leaning when low ?

Yes, you can start learning even when feeling low. Begin with small, manageable tasks to build momentum. Learning can actually boost your mood by providing a sense of achievement and distraction from negative thoughts.

how to come out of depression ?

To come out of depression, it's important to seek professional help, such as therapy or medication. Additionally, engage in activities that uplift you, like regular exercise, connecting with loved ones, maintaining a routine, and practicing mindfulness. Small steps, like setting achievable goals, can also help in gradually improving your mood.
